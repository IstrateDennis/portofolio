Urban Agriculture Center

Conversion of a former cigarette factory into a community-oriented agriculture and education hub. The project integrates greenhouses, classrooms, a market hall, and a cafe. Strategy emphasizes minimal structural intervention and reversible details.

  • Area: 12,400 m² — phased implementation
  • Structure: existing concrete frames + light timber infill
  • Performance: daylight-first, greywater reuse, passive ventilation
Riverfront Cultural Pavilion

Modular timber pavilion with operable louver walls enabling year-round use. The grid allows reconfiguration for performances, markets, and workshops.

  • Material: locally sourced glulam + polycarbonate
  • Assembly: dry connections for reuse
Courtyard Housing Prototype

Courtyard clusters balance privacy and community. Units cross-ventilate and share winter gardens. Facades use low-cost terracotta screens.
Material Reuse Atlas

City-wide dataset of components available for reuse from scheduled demolitions, with routing to local workshops. Output: atlas + digital map.
Library in a Park

A sequence of reading rooms under a green roof. Structure and landscape interlock to create shade and rainwater retention.
Timber Micro-Units
